About the Author

Tabiti Kehinde is a seasoned researcher, consultant , business administrator dedicated teacher,
and inspiring speaker who has spent years delving into the complexities of human behavior, resilience, and personal growth.
With a deep passion for understanding life’s challenges and how individuals overcome them,
the author has combined extensive academic research with real-world insights to craft a book that speaks to the heart of human perseverance.
Drawing on a wealth of experience author has walked many of the same paths explored in this book.
Both the highs and lows of personal and professional life have shaped their perspective on endurance and success.
